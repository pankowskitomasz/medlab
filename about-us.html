About us

Why laboratory diagnostics

Our mission is to make actionable information accessible to everyone at the time it matters

By making actionable information accessible to everyone at the time it matters most, we are working to develop early detection and prevention of disease and empower people everywhere to own their health and live their best possible lifes.

Medlab

The genesis of the company

The word Medlab originated from the integration of medical and laboratory

The current definition of diagnosis is 'The act of indetifying a disease from its signs and symptoms' Which is the problem. Because in many cases the presence of these symptoms means it is too late.

Medlab is a pioneer in application of AI in predictive diagnostics

So we can to begin build the world where no one finds out too late that something is wrong.

It's your body, it's your health data

You own your data. We will never use your information for purposes other than required to deliver you diagnostic results.

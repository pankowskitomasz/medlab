How to register by phone

Before calling our consultants please prepare appropriate information. Our consultant will ask for following information about you:

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Birth date
  • Social Security Number
  • Address
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • List of tests you need to make

When you will be ready, please call our registration center:

+(01) 0-800 273-456-234

Our consultant will ask you for your personal data and the tests you need to make. Basing on this information our consultant will provide you information about the localization of nearest to you testing centers. When you choose location, you will receive list of available terms. After choosing one our employee will make reservation for you and send you email with reservation details. It's done, your reservation was made!

How to register online

In order to register online please fill the registration form below. Shortly after you send it you will receive email in which you will be asked to confirm your request. It's done, your reservation was made!
